User Data Deletion Instructions

Daily XI does not store your personal data on our servers. However, if you wish to remove the app's access to your Facebook account, follow these steps:

  1. Go to your Facebook Account's Settings & Privacy. Click Settings.
  2. Look for Apps and Websites in the left menu.
  3. Find Daily XI and click Remove.
  4. If you wish to request a manual purge of any cached metadata, please email [ibrahimdar1986@gmail.com].
